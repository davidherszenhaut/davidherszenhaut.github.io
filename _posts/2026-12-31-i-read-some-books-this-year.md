Welcome to my very professional and very in-depth thoughts on the books I read in 2026.

**住野よるの『また、同じ夢を見ていた』**: これは日本語で小説を読むこと初めてでしたが、どんな面白くなかった本でした。でも、たくさん新しい言葉とフレーズを見たし日本語の生徒に勧めるのは理解出来ました。3/5.

***The Fisherman* by John Langan**: I really liked the pacing of this book. It always felt like it was moving forward and gave just enough details to flesh out the story without getting bogged down. Or maybe I just feel that way because the last book I read was in a language I don't speak and it took me longer to get through it than it probably should have. What I do know for sure is that I quite liked how the idea of a world like ours (but not exactly) existing underneath us mirrored the story within a story structure, and how that was also reflected in the physical description of the creek and its surroundings. 4/5.
